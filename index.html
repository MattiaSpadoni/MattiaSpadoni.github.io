The relic

Now it's difficult for us to imagine how a relic like this was produced. Indeed vases like the one in front of you were quite famous in Roman age.

Craftmen and slaves worked day after day to produce ceramic objects. The one in front of you was produced in the area of the Forum before its construction.

2 vase workshops were present on the actual Forum location. This vase was cooked, but then destroyed due to its deformation. Restorers rebuilt.

This vase specifically is from the late republican age, dating back to I century BC.

P.S. The numbers you can see are not anciet, they are made by archaeologist to catalogue relics.
