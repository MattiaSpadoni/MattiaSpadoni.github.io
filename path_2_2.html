The relic

Little pieces of metal? What are them? Why are they in this path?

The answer is: they are all that remain of ancient footwear. Romans didn't have sintetic materials, they had to use organic ones to make shoes.

Made mainly by leather, Roman shoes were of 2 types: closed like boots or open like sandals in many cases decorated with buckles and striples made by precious materials.

Nails were used in the shoe sole and they left clear footsteps in the the terrain. These nails you can see are what remain of an ancient shoe.
